About

The recruiter
who builds things.

My background spans technical recruiting, people operations, and workflow automation across fast-moving organizations. I specialize in sourcing engineering talent through signal-based methodologies rather than keyword search, and in building the internal tooling that makes recruiting teams more effective.

The sourcing toolkit on this page was built from scratch using Node.js and the GitHub API, with zero external dependencies. It reflects the kind of systems thinking I bring to people operations work — identifying a problem, understanding the underlying data, and building a repeatable solution around it.

I am particularly interested in the intersection of AI, assessment science, and talent acquisition, and the opportunity to build people technology that makes hiring more accurate, equitable, and human.
